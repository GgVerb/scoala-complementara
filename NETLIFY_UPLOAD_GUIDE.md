# Deploy to Netlify - Simplified Guide

## Step 1: Download the Ready Site
- In Replit file explorer, find `scoalaverb-netlify-ready.zip`
- Click to download this file (contains your complete website)

## Step 2: Deploy to Netlify
1. Go to https://netlify.com
2. Sign up for free account
3. Click "Deploy manually" or "Drag and drop your site folder here"
4. Upload the `scoalaverb-netlify-ready.zip` file
5. Netlify will automatically extract and deploy your site

## Step 3: Customize Your URL
- In Netlify dashboard → Site settings → General
- Change site name from random name to: `scoalaverb`
- Your site URL becomes: `scoalaverb.netlify.app`
